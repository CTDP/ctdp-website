International Formula Masters

A free game addon for Assetto Corsa

The premiere mod of CTDP for Assetto Corsa brings you the complete 2009 season with all cars that competed over the full year.

Look all the realism

The International Formula Master (IFM) was a supporting series during the European WTCC races from 2007 till 2009. The series was a junior Formula series which was thought as a stepping stone into the GP2. The cars were manufactured by Italian based company Tatuus and were comparable to smaller single seaters like the Formula 3 cars.

Such physics,
very wow!

We got our hands on the original user manual for the car we have access to so many real life data as never before in our history. That helps us in creating highly realistic physics for IFM 2009 so you can feel like a real race driver.

And stuff!

The addon comes with full setup guide, templates and texture viewer.

About

CTDP is the short for Cars & Tracks Development Project. We are an independant modding-group for simracing mods. We create high quality content for racing simulations and released several full-season addons for games such as F1 Challenge and rFactor.

Our members are all motorsports enthusiasts and we do modding as a hobby. CTDP's work is strictly non-profit oriented and non-commercial.
We just love it.
